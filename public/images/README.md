# Vehicle Images

## How to Add Your Vehicle Photos

### Step 1: Take or Gather Photos
Take high-quality photos of your vehicles:
- **Cadillac XT6** - Black exterior, preferably side/front angle
- **Ford Flex** - Black exterior, preferably side/front angle
- **Lincoln MKT** - Black exterior, preferably side/front angle
- **Hero image** - One stunning photo of your best vehicle (Cadillac XT6 recommended)

### Step 2: Name Your Photos
Rename your photos exactly as follows:
- `hero/cadillac-xt6-hero.jpg` - Main hero background image
- `fleet/cadillac-xt6.jpg` - Cadillac XT6 for fleet section
- `fleet/ford-flex.jpg` - Ford Flex for fleet section
- `fleet/lincoln-mkt.jpg` - Lincoln MKT for fleet section

### Step 3: Add Photos to Project
Place the photos in these folders:
- Hero image: `public/images/hero/`
- Fleet images: `public/images/fleet/`

### Photo Requirements:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Resolution**: At least 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Orientation**: Horizontal/landscape
- **Quality**: High quality, well-lit, professional-looking
- **Background**: Clean, uncluttered backgrounds work best

### After Adding Photos:
The website will automatically use your photos instead of stock images. Refresh your browser to see the changes!
